# 3-Day Fast Protocol

**Purpose:** Operational hour-by-hour guide for a 72-hour fast. Scaled for 135 kg body mass and Thai heat.

**Reference:** `claude-knowledge-base/projects/fitness-rewards/nutrition-plan/FAST-DAY-ROUTINE.md` (strategic layer) and `ELECTROLYTES.md` (targets).

**Status:** Active fast starting at hour 0 below.

---

## Hour 0 stock check

Confirm all of these are in reach before starting. Missing items derail the fast on day 2.

- [ ] Pink/sea salt (need 30-50g over 3 days)
- [ ] Lite salt / potassium chloride (เกลือโลว์โซเดียม)
- [ ] Magnesium glycinate 400mg — minimum 3 caps
- [ ] Chicken broth — homemade or Knorr cubes (MSG is fine; it's sodium)
- [ ] Limes
- [ ] Bottled/filtered water — 4 L/day minimum
- [ ] Black coffee, green/oolong tea

---

## Daily protocol — all 3 days

| Time | Action | Dose |
|---|---|---|
| 06:00 | Wake. Electrolyte drink #1 | 1 tsp pink salt + ¼ tsp lite salt + 500ml water + lime |
| 07:00 | Black coffee + water | 500ml water |
| 10:00 | Plain water | 500ml |
| 11:00 | Electrolyte drink #2 | Same as #1 |
| 14:00 | Chicken broth + pinch extra salt | 300-400ml |
| 15:00 | Plain water | 500ml |
| 17:00 | Electrolyte drink #3 | Same as #1 |
| 20:00 | Broth + magnesium glycinate | 300ml broth, 400mg Mg |
| Bed | Sleep. Extra pillow if HR feels high. | — |

**Daily totals:** ~7,000mg sodium · ~2,400mg potassium · 400mg Mg · 4+ L water.

If the day is unusually hot, add one extra electrolyte drink between 11:00 and 17:00. Sweat potassium loss is real in Lamphun.

---

## What to expect per day

### Day 1
- Hunger waves until ~hour 18, then fades
- Mild afternoon headache likely → means under-salted, add a pinch
- Ketone breath develops overnight

### Day 2 (hardest)
- Hunger mostly gone
- Energy dips 14:00-16:00 — push through with broth + walk
- **Optional escape valve:** 100-150g plain boiled chicken breast at 14:00 if energy crashes
  - Breaks autophagy, keeps ketosis, preserves muscle
  - Not a failure — it's a documented tool in your protocol

### Day 3
- Ketones 2-4 mmol/L
- Clear-headed, not hungry, slightly euphoric
- Adaptation marker hit

---

## Movement

- **No 5x5.** Walking is fine. Sauna if available — stacks well with fasted ketosis.
- **Don't sweat heavily outdoors midday.** Thai heat + low sodium is the faint risk.
- Morning or evening walks only.

---

## Abort conditions

Stop and eat if any of these:

| Signal | Meaning | Action |
|---|---|---|
| HR >110 bpm at rest after electrolytes | Under-mineralized | Eat something light, end the fast |
| Dizzy standing past day 1 | Orthostatic hypotension | Salt + broth; if not fixed in 30 min, eat |
| Can't sleep night 2 | Often magnesium deficit | Extra Mg; if persistent, end |
| Dark yellow urine despite 4L water | Kidneys in distress | Break fast |

Know these thresholds in advance. Don't negotiate with yourself at 3am.

---

## Breaking the fast — most important step

**The fast ends at hour 72, not at the next meal.** Refeed is where the gains get undone.

### Hour 72 break (~19:00 day 3)

- 2 eggs, soft-scrambled in coconut oil
- ½ avocado
- 50-100g plain protein (leftover chicken, broth with meat)
- Pinch of salt

**That's it.** Gut is idle after 72h. Villi atrophied, digestion slow. A big meal here causes:
- Bloating, reflux
- **Refeeding syndrome risk:** rapid electrolyte shifts, magnesium/phosphate drop — serious at your body mass after a long fast

### Hour 76 (~23:00 day 3) or Day 4 morning

- Bigger keto meal, normal structure
- No rice, no sugar, no processed food
- You paid 3 days for adaptation — protect it for at least a week

### Day 4 onward

Follow your weekly meal plan. Return to 5x5 Day 5 (Friday if fast ends Wed) with the light-ramp weights in `FAST-DAY-ROUTINE.md`.

---

## Weight expectations

- 3-5 kg drop over 3 days (~70% water/glycogen)
- Settles ~2 kg below starting weight after refeed
- Don't treat day-3 scale reading as "real" fat loss

---

## Key reminders

- **Salt more than feels right.** Under-salting is how fasts fail.
- **Broth is your friend on day 2.** Use it freely.
- **Mg before bed every night.** Non-negotiable.
- **4+ L water daily.** Even if not thirsty.
- **No workout.** Walking yes, lifting no.
- **Abort without shame** if abort conditions hit. Modified fast or refeed is better than a hospital trip.
