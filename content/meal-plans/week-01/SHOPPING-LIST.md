## ⚠️ Avoid at Makro (common traps)

- **Ranch / Caesar / Thousand Island dressings** — full of sugar, vegetable oil
- **Pre-packaged "keto" snacks** — usually marketing; read labels
- **Most Thai curry pastes** — many brands add palm sugar; look for **"ไม่เติมน้ำตาล"** (no added sugar) or make your own
- **Fruit juices, "coconut water"** — concentrated sugar
- **Sweetened yogurt drinks** — Dutch Mill etc., all sugar
- **Low-fat anything** — fat is the point
- **Standard sriracha** — check for added sugar; the "no sugar added" versions are better
