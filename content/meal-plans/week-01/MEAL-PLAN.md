# Weekly Meal Plan — Week 1

**Style:** 16:8 keto, dairy-free, strict (<20g net carbs/day)
**Structure:** Meal 1 at 12:00 (bigger, post-workout on training days), Meal 2 at 18:00 (lighter). Window closes 20:00.
**Special weekly features:** Sun dinner → Mon dinner 24h fast. Thursday optional 18:6 or 24h.
**Target macros:** Training day 2,600 kcal / 190g protein / 185g fat / <20g nc. Rest day 2,400 kcal / 180g protein / 175g fat / <20g nc.

All recipes are from `/content/recipes/` (see `INDEX.md`) and are dairy-free. Each recipe serving as written = 1/4 of the full recipe. "2x" below means eat half the pot. "1.5x" means eat ~37% of the pot (Maleewan or another person can have the rest).

**New for this week:** 19 Whole30-friendly recipes added from tasteslovely.com, 40aprons.com, realsimplegood.com, paleoglutenfreeguy.com, nyssaskitchen.com, thismomsmenu.com, and eatbeautiful.net. The plan rotates in the most useful ones to add variety.

## Rules of thumb when substituting

- If a recipe calls for heavy cream → use **coconut cream** (UHT from Makro)
- If it calls for butter → use **coconut oil** or **ghee** (clarified butter is technically dairy-free)
- If it calls for cheese → **skip** or substitute with more avocado/nuts
- If it calls for yogurt → use **coconut yogurt** (harder to find; ask at Rimping)
- If meal is under-caloric → add more oil, avocado, or macadamia nuts
- If meal is under-protein → add 2 boiled eggs (12g protein each)

## What's NOT in this plan but you may want

These are Makro staples that don't appear in any single recipe but are helpful to have:
- **Pork rinds (แคบหมู)** — zero-carb crunch when you crave chips
- **Sardines / mackerel tins** — emergency protein, 25g per tin
- **Hard-boiled eggs** — batch-cook 12 on Saturday
- **Macadamia nuts / pecans** — highest fat-to-carb ratio nut
- **85% dark chocolate** — small squares, low carb
- **Olives** — fat + salt
- **MCT oil** — coffee boost on training mornings
