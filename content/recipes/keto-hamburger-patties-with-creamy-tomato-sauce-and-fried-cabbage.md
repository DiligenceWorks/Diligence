# Keto hamburger patties with creamy tomato sauce and fried cabbage

A delicious burger needs no bun! Enjoy this satisfying bunless patty topped with creamy tomato gravy and a side of sautéed cabbage. Keto satisfaction guaranteed!

**Source:** [dietdoctor.com](https://www.dietdoctor.com/recipes/keto-hamburger-patties-tomato-sauce-cabbage)

## Macros (per serving)
- **Calories:** 685 kcal
- **Protein:** 41 g
- **Fat:** 52 g
- **Net carbs:** 5 g (total 9g − fiber 4g)

## Time
- Prep: 20 min
- Cook: 20 min
- Total: 40 min
- Servings: 4

## Ingredients
- 1 1/2 lbs ground beef
- 1 egg
- 3 oz. feta cheese
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp ground black pepper
- 2 1/4 oz. fresh parsley
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 3/4 cup heavy whipping cream
- 2 tbsp tomato paste
- pepper
- 1 1/4 lbs green cabbage
- 2 tbsp butter

## Instructions
1. Combine all the ingredients for the hamburgers in a large bowl using a wooden spoon or clean hands. Don&#039;t over-mix since that can make the patties tough. Use wet hands to form oblong patties.
2. Add olive oil or butter to a large frying pan. Fry over medium-high heat for at least 10 minutes or until the patties have browned. Flip them a few times for even cooking.
3. In a small bowl, whisk together the tomato paste and cream. Add this mixture to the pan when the patties are almost done. Stir and let simmer for a few minutes. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
4. Sprinkle with chopped parsley before serving.
5. Shred the cabbage finely using a food processor or sharp knife.
6. Add butter to a large frying pan.
7. Place the pan over medium-high heat and sauté the shredded cabbage for at least 15 minutes or until the cabbage is wilted and golden brown around the edges.
8. Stir regularly and lower the heat a little towards the end. Add salt and pepper to taste.
