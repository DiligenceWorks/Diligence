# Crustless keto broccoli and bacon quiche

This quick and easy quiche only uses a handful of ingredients, yet it's certainly not lacking in flavor. A rich, creamy quiche that can easily make a wholesome breakfast, lunch, or dinner.

**Source:** [dietdoctor.com](https://www.dietdoctor.com/recipes/crustless-keto-broccoli-and-bacon-quiche)

## Macros (per serving)
- **Calories:** 509 kcal
- **Protein:** 32 g
- **Fat:** 40 g
- **Net carbs:** 4 g (total 5g − fiber 1g)

## Time
- Prep: 10 min
- Cook: 30 min
- Total: 40 min
- Servings: 4

## Ingredients
- 1 tsp butter, for greasing
- 4 oz. bacon
- 8 eggs
- 1/2 cup heavy whipping cream
- 1/2 tbsp Italian seasoning
- 1/2 tbsp onion powder
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 4 oz. broccoli, cut into florets
- 6 oz. mozzarella cheese

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 350 F° (175 C°). Grease a 9&quot; (23 cm) pie plate with butter.
2. Place the bacon in a cold skillet. Cook over medium heat until crispy, turning occasionally to brown evenly. Drain the bacon on a paper towel-lined plate, and then cut the bacon into small pieces. Set aside.
3. In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the eggs, cream, and seasonings until well combined.
4. Add the broccoli and bacon pieces to the pie plate. Pour the egg mixture over the broccoli and bacon and sprinkle the cheese on top.
5. Bake on the middle rack for 20-30 minutes, or until golden brown. Cool for 5 minutes before serving.
