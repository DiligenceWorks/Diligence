# Asian keto chicken stir-fry with broccoli

Need a low carb meal in a few minutes? This fast and easy Asian keto stir fry has just five ingredients: boneless chicken thighs, broccoli, tamari soy sauce, garlic, and olive oil. And only 5 grams of net carbs. Simple, delicious, and keto. Serve with spicy mayo.

**Source:** [dietdoctor.com](https://www.dietdoctor.com/recipes/asian-chicken-stir-fry-with-broccoli)

## Macros (per serving)
- **Calories:** 613 kcal
- **Protein:** 43 g
- **Fat:** 46 g
- **Net carbs:** 3 g (total 5g − fiber 2g)

## Time
- Prep: 5 min
- Cook: 20 min
- Total: 25 min
- Servings: 4

## Ingredients
- 3/4 cup mayonnaise
- 2 tbsp hot sauce
- 2 tsp garlic powder
- 1 3/4 lbs boneless chicken thighs
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp ground black pepper
- 10 oz. broccoli
- 2 tbsp tamari soy sauce

## Instructions
1. Add the ingredients to a small bowl, and stir to combine.
2. Heat the olive oil in a wok or large frying pan, over medium-high heat. Add the chicken, garlic powder, and pepper. Stir-fry for a few minutes, until the chicken turns golden brown.
3. Add the broccoli and tamari soy sauce. Stir together for a couple of minutes until the broccoli is crisp, yet tender. Spoon into individual bowls, and serve with the spicy mayonnaise on the side.
