# Keto baked salmon with lemon and butter

Place this keto platter on the table and watch the crowd go gaga for lemony salmon! And to guild the lily, there’s lemon butter too. Doing salmon right? Oh, yeah.

**Source:** [dietdoctor.com](https://www.dietdoctor.com/recipes/keto-baked-salmon-lemon-butter)

## Macros (per serving)
- **Calories:** 574 kcal
- **Protein:** 31 g
- **Fat:** 49 g
- **Net carbs:** 1 g (total 1g − fiber 0g)

## Time
- Prep: 10 min
- Cook: 25 min
- Total: 35 min
- Servings: 6

## Ingredients
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 2 lbs salmon, boneless fillets
- 1 tsp sea salt
- ground black pepper
- 7 oz. butter, thinly & equally sliced, divided
- 1 lemon, sliced thinly
- 2 tbsp lemon juice, or juice from 1 lemon

## Instructions
1. Place the salmon, skin-side down in the prepared baking dish. Cover the fillets with the lemon slices, and top each lemon slice, with a thin, butter slice.
2. Bake on the middle rack for about 20–30 minutes, or until the salmon is opaque and flakes easily with a fork.
3. Serve with lemon butter and a side dish of your choice. See below for suggestions.
