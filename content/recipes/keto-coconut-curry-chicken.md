# Keto coconut curry chicken

Imagine a silky coconut sauce filled with fragrant lemongrass, warming ginger, and a kick of heat from the curry. Oh, yes. It’s real, and it’s happening.

**Source:** [dietdoctor.com](https://www.dietdoctor.com/recipes/keto-curry-chicken-coconut-lime)

## Macros (per serving)
- **Calories:** 721 kcal
- **Protein:** 50 g
- **Fat:** 54 g
- **Net carbs:** 3 g (total 8g − fiber 5g)

## Time
- Prep: 20 min
- Cook: 20 min
- Total: 40 min
- Servings: 4

## Ingredients
- 2 stalks of lemongrass
- 3 tbsp coconut oil
- 2 tbsp fresh ginger, finely chopped
- 2 garlic cloves, finely chopped
- 2 tbsp curry powder or green curry paste
- 1 red chili pepper, finely chopped
- 2 lbs boneless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
- salt, to taste
- 1 leek, chopped
- 1/2 red bell pepper, sliced
- 14 oz. coconut cream
- 1 lime, the finely grated zest
- 1/2 cup fresh cilantro

## Instructions
1. Crush the tough part of the lemongrass with the broad side of a knife or pestle. Chop it finely.
2. Heat the coconut oil in a large wok or a frying pan over medium-high heat. Add the lemongrass, ginger, garlic, curry, and chili. Sauté for a couple of minutes before adding the chicken. Season with salt. Fry until golden.
3. Add the leek and bell pepper and give them a good mix. Sauté for a couple of minutes. The vegetables should still be crunchy.
4. Add the coconut cream and let simmer for 5–10 minutes until everything is warm.
5. Top with cilantro and sprinkle over the lime zest before serving.
