# Keto Buffalo chicken with paprika mayo and butter-fried cabbage

Everyday deliciousness, pure and simple. Juicy chicken thighs, buttered cabbage, plus a flavored mayo — either spicy or not-so-spicy. It's an easy keto meal that you'll make again and again.

**Source:** [dietdoctor.com](https://www.dietdoctor.com/recipes/buffalo-chicken-paprika-mayo-butter-fried-cabbage)

## Macros (per serving)
- **Calories:** 856 kcal
- **Protein:** 41 g
- **Fat:** 71 g
- **Net carbs:** 3 g (total 8g − fiber 5g)

## Time
- Prep: 10 min
- Cook: 50 min
- Total: 60 min
- Servings: 4

## Ingredients
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp garlic powder
- 2 1/4 tbsp hot sauce
- 1 tsp salt
- 2 lbs chicken thighs (bone-in with skin)
- 1 1/2 lbs green cabbage
- 1 oz. butter
- pepper
- 1/2 cup mayonnaise
- 1 tbsp paprika powder

## Instructions
1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
2. Mix olive oil, garlic powder, hot sauce, and salt in a large bowl. Add chicken thighs and stir to cover; alternatively, brush the mixture all over the thighs.
3. Place the chicken in a greased baking dish with the skin side up. Bake in the oven for about 40-50 minutes or until the chicken is thoroughly cooked. If you use a thermometer it should read 165°F (75°C) when ready.
4. In the meantime, shred the cabbage finely with a knife or food processor.
5. Melt butter in a skillet and fry cabbage over medium heat until soft and golden brown around the edges. Salt and pepper to taste.
6. In a small bowl, mix mayonnaise, paprika powder, and hot sauce or tabasco. Combine well.
7. Serve the chicken and cabbage with a dollop of flavored mayonnaise and perhaps an extra dash of hot sauce if you like, enjoy.
