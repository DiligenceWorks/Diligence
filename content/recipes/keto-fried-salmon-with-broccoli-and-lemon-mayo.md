# Keto fried salmon with broccoli and lemon mayo

Elegant salmon. Bright, vibrant broccoli. Lemon mayo. Now that’s what we call a one-skillet wonder. And it’s keto elegance at its finest.

**Source:** [dietdoctor.com](https://www.dietdoctor.com/recipes/keto-fried-salmon-broccoli-lemon-mayo)

## Macros (per serving)
- **Calories:** 780 kcal
- **Protein:** 44 g
- **Fat:** 63 g
- **Net carbs:** 2 g (total 5g − fiber 3g)

## Time
- Prep: 10 min
- Cook: 15 min
- Total: 25 min
- Servings: 4

## Ingredients
- 3/4 cup mayonnaise
- 2 tbsp lemon juice
- 1 lb broccoli
- 1 3/4 lbs salmon, boneless fillets
- salt
- 1 oz. butter
- pepper

## Instructions
1. Mix mayonnaise and lemon juice. Set aside for later.
2. Divide the salmon into serving-sized pieces. Season with salt and pepper.
3. Rinse and trim the broccoli, including the stem. Chop into bite-sized pieces.
4. Fry the salmon in half the butter or oil over medium heat for a couple of minutes on each side. Lower the heat towards the end. Remove from pan and keep warm.
5. Add the remaining butter or oil to the same skillet and cook the broccoli over medium heat for 3-4 minutes or until it is slightly softened and golden brown. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
6. Serve the fried salmon and broccoli together with a hearty dollop of lemon mayo.
